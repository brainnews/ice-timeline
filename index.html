ICE Training Deficiencies

A documented timeline of warnings, failures, and consequences

Summary

Since ICE's creation in 2003, government watchdogs have repeatedly warned about training deficiencies. These documented warnings about inadequate training, civil rights instruction gaps, and oversight failures have materialized in incidents of excessive force, constitutional violations, and fatal shootings.
